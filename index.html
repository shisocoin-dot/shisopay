📖 About ShisoPay

ShisoPay is a decentralized crypto payment gateway built for merchants and businesses to accept SHISO and other cryptocurrency payments securely and instantly. By leveraging blockchain technology, ShisoPay ensures fast, transparent, and low-fee transactions worldwide.

  • 💻 Easy integration with websites, e-commerce platforms, and POS systems.
  • 🎁 Loyalty and reward programs for customers.

⚙️ How ShisoPay Works

  1. Merchants register on the ShisoPay platform and receive a unique payment ID.
  2. Integrate ShisoPay checkout button or API on your website or store.
  3. Customers choose crypto payment and scan QR code or use wallet link.
  4. Transaction is verified on blockchain and merchant receives instant payment.
  5. All payments are recorded in the merchant dashboard for tracking and accounting.
